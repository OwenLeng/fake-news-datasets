'''Karol Martesko-Fenster''' is an Austrian-born American entrepreneur and pioneering cross media executive. He has been active in the motion picture, publishing, music television and Internet industries for over two decades and includes leadership in the American independent film industry. Currently the Senior Vice-President/General Manager of Babel Networks US, Karol leads the film division for [[Babelgum]] the global web, mobile and IPTV destination. Karol joined Babel Networks in September of 2008 after providing strategic consulting services to numerous film and media ventures, including Arts Alliance, FILMHAUS Vienna, T5M, Tribeca Enterprises, CINELAN and The WorkBook Project.
