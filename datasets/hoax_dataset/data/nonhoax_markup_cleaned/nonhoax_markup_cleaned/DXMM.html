DXMM is an AM radio station owned by the Apostolic Vicariate of Jolo of the Roman Catholic Church. It is found on 927 khz. on the AM band. It is the fruit of missions made by the Missionary Oblates of Mary Immaculate in the difficult and harsh fields of Mindanao and Sulu archipelago in southern Philippines. DXMM is broadcasting rooted on the tenets of the Church and is geared towards promoting inter-religious dialogue among the tri-people of Mindanao.
