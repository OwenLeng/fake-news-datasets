The season began with the first regular season games in September 2010 and ended with the play-off final in May 2011. [[S.P. Tre Fiori|Tre Fiori]] were the defending league champions, having won their sixth Sammarinese championship and second-in-a-row last season. ==Participating teams== Because there is no promotion or relegation in the league, the same 15 teams who competed in the league [[2009–10 Campionato Sammarinese di Calcio|last season]] competed in the league this season.
