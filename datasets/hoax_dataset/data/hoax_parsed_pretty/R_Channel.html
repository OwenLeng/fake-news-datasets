R Channel is a commercial terrestrial television in Malaysia. It was officially launched it started began broadcasting nationwide on 10 December 2011 as R Channel is known as the first free-to-air television station in Malaysia formatted as variety and entertainment television channel similar to BBC Entertainment (United Kingdom), China Entertainment Television (Hong Kong), MNC Entertainment (Indonesia) and CCTV-3 (People's Republic of China). R Channel broadcasts for 12-hours in daily from noon to midnight.

The channel usually sign-on of opening ceremony of transmission begins are inaugurated broadcasts at noon and sign-off in closing ceremony of transmission ends are final broadcasts at midnight in daily.
