Albion Dauti (born May 31, 1995 in Caracas, Venezuela) is a Venezuelan telenovela actor and presenter.

Biography[edit]

Albion Dauti was born in Caracas, Venezuela. He studied acting at the Faculty of Arts in Caracas. In 2010 he started working as a presenter in Venevision.

Personal career[edit]

He plays in Solo tu y yo (Only You And Me), a Venevision telenovela, sharing roles with Berta Bajrami and Hilda Abrahamz, it will be released in 2014.

Telenovelas[edit]

  • 2014: Solo tu y yo as Albert Mendoza(co-protagonist)
  • 2013: Amor y sufrimiento as Juan Miguel( protagonist)
