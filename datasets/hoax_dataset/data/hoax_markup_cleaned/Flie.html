Flie Marquel is a character from the book [[Les Miserables]]. She is the daughter of the rich merchant Pierre Marquel. She ran away to join the reveloution when her father hired a governess for her. She went disguised as a boy. She befriends the street urchin [[Gavroche]], who is oblivious to the fact that she is a girl. She and Gavroche helped gather new students to fight in the revolution. When Gavroche was being shot by enemy forces, Flie selflessly rushed out, saved him and brought him to the hospital.
