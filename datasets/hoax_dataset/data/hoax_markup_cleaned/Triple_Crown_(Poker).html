{{Proposed deletion/dated |concern = Made up, not a recognized poker concept; in fact, triple crown in a poker context generally means a different concept "to win three different tournaments on three different online sites over the course of one week" |timestamp = 20150324204438 }} The '''Triple Crown of Poker''' is made up of the [[European Poker Tour]], the [[World Poker Tour]] and a [[World Series of Poker]]. In order to complete the Triple Crown of Poker you must win an EPT main event, a WPT main event and a WSOP main or side event. To date only five individuals have accomplished this feat.
