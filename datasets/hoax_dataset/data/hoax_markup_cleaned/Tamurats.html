{{db-hoax}} {{unreferenced|date=February 2008}} The '''Tamurats''' ({{lang-tr|Tamuratlar}}) are an [[organized crime|organised]], [[Sicilian Mafia|Mafia]]-like crime group operating in mainly [[Istanbul]], [[Turkey]]. They are a gang still shrouded in mystery but records suggest that they began as early as 1927. Like many other organised crime groups they have a structure in which has a boss at the top, then many underbosses, then soldiers and lastly street gangs. Many street gangs are under the influence of the Tamurats and carry out most of the violent crime. {{Organized crime groups in Asia}} {{crime-org-stub}} {{wikipedia-deleted|Mttll}}
