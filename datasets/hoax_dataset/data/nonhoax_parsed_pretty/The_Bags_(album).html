The Bags, often referred to as The Bags LP due to its untitled cover and spine, is the third album by The Bags. This album was later re-released on Compact Disc in 2008 by the Oaf Records and Heartworm Music labels, with additional tracks from the Stanton '89 single, "I Know"/"Hide and Seek", and a couple of previously unreleased tracks.
