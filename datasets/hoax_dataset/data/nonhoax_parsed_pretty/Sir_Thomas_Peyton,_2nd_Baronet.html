Sir Thomas Peyton, 2nd Baronet

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sir Thomas Peyton, 2nd Baronet (18 August 1613 - 11 February 1684) was an English politician. He was a Member of Parliament (MP) for Kent from 1661 to 1679.[1]

He died aged 70.
