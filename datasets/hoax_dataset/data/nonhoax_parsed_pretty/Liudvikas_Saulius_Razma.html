Liudvikas Saulius Razma

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Liudvikas Saulius Razma (born February 7, 1938) is a Lithuanian politician. In 1990 he was among those who signed the Act of the Re-Establishment of the State of Lithuania.
